Welcome to Data Science Club

Event: BINARY BRAINS

Rounds: 2

Description:

Round 1: Qualification Round

The “Binary Brains Competition” is centred around quizzes that challenge participants with multiple-choice questions on the fundamentals of data science and Python programming.

DURATION : 1 Hour

Round 2: Final Round

The competition is focused on error identification in Python, specifically involving conditional statements, loops, and fundamental Python concepts.

DURATION : 1 Hour

Judging Criteria

Judged based upon their basic python skills and knowledge on Data Science.

Event: CODE CRUSADE

Rounds: 2

Description:

Round 1: Qualification Round

In this round, students will write Python code to generate and display specified patterns, demonstrating their understanding of loops and logic.

DURATION : 1 Hour 30 Minutes

Round 2: Final Round

In this round, students have to create dashboards using PowerBI software on the datasets provided in the Excel format.

DURATION : 2 Hours

Judging Criteria

Judged based upon their advanced skills in python and PowerBi dashboards.

Event: DATA WIZARDS

Rounds: 2

Description:

Round 1: Qualification Round

The 'Code Identification and Rearrangement' round. In this round, students reassemble jumbled code to form a functioning program. The event tests coding syntax, logic, and practical application skills.

DURATION : 1 Hour

Round 2: Final Round

Data Diving Activity: Participants start by analysing complex datasets, testing their ability to understand and interpret detailed data, a crucial skill in data science. Visualization Voyage Activity: Participants create clear and engaging visualizations to highlight key insights from the data, assessing their ability to use visuals effectively in data analysis.

DURATION : 2 Hours 30 Minutes

Judging Criteria

Judged based upon their advanced skills in Data Science , like Data Visualization and Machine Learning modules.
