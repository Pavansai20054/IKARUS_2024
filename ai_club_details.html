Event: CODE CRACK
Levels: 2
Description:

Level 1: Mathematics Logic Problems

Your task is to find and fix these errors to ensure the code runs correctly. This level tests your understanding of mathematical operations, logic, and Python syntax.

Level 2: Functions and Code Analysis

In the second level, you'll encounter more complex code focused on Python functions. You'll need to identify errors related to function definitions, parameters, return statements, and more. This level is designed to challenge you deeper.
Event: PREDICTIVE POWERHOUSE: The Ultimate ML Design Duel
Levels: 3
Description:

Level 1: Project Expo

On the first day, participants will showcase their machine learning projects through a visually captivating poster presentation. This level tests their ability to communicate complex ideas succinctly and effectively.

Level 2: Presentation and Execution

Still on Day 1, those who excel in the Project Expo will advance to the next round, where they will deliver a detailed presentation of their project, followed by a live demonstration. This level evaluates both their depth of knowledge and technical execution.

Level 3: Challenge Development

On Day 2, the finalists will face a twist—judges will introduce a new challenge or modification to their project. Participants must adapt and develop their solutions by evening, proving their creativity and problem-solving skills under pressure. The winner will be crowned as the ultimate ML Design Champion.
